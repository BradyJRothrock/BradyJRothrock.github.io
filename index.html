Brady Rothrock

Brady Rothrock joined Sprint in 2012 and currently works as a Data Scientist within the Internal Audit Technology Team. He performs and guides data analytics projects in audit areas spanning retail, finance, operations, and enterprise risk management.

Prior to joining Sprint, he obtained his Certified Public Account (CPA) license and Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE) designation before entering the field of data analysis. He serves as a local user group leader for an industry specific analytics tool, as well as the Technology Director for the Kansas City Association of Certified Fraud Examiners. His spare time is spent researching Machine Learning models and Sabermetrics (the application of statistical analysis to baseball data).
